## Can't connect wallet - Chrome browser

#### Problem:

Try to click **Contect wallet** button, but nothing happens.

#### Solution:

Clear cache & cookies of Chrome browser:

1. Open chrome dev-tools
2. Go to **Application** tab
3. Go to **Storage**
4. Click **Clear site data**
